lloyd hilaiel

i build software.

My passion is creating software that matters with people who care - technology has been my hobby for the last 30 years, and my profession for 18 of those.

Professionally, I've worked for startups which you've probably never heard of, the University of California, Yahoo!, and Mozilla. I have been a consultant, a teacher, an evangelist, a principal engineer, and a director. I've led teams of four, and teams of twenty. I am the creator and maintainer of several popular open source libraries.

I lead from the front, follow when well led, came to party, and bring optimism and energy to the room.

Personally, I'm the proud father of two, the dedicated husband of one, and currently reside in Sofia, Bulgaria. Also lately, I like to run.

On this humble website, you'll find my blog. You can peruse or use my open source work on github. If you want to contact me, twitter or email work equally well.

Onward!
